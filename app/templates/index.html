What is MedICI

The Medical Imaging Challenge Infrastructure (MedICI) promotes innovation through collaboration and harness the ingenuity of the public. It is based on the Microsoft open-source project CodaLab and integrates the specialized image software such as ePad for DICOM images and caMicroscope for digital pathology whole-slide images. Other customizations were incorporated based on user input.
